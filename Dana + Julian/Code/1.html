Tell Us How We Are Doing!

We couldn't provide the excellent level of service to our communities if it wasn't for YOU! Near the anniversary of your start date with the Red Cross, you'll receive a Volunteer Satisfaction Survey by email. The survey will have a number of questions to see how we're doing at being the premier volunteer organization. On question number six, we want you to be able to rate us a "9" or "10".

If for any reason you can't choose a "9" or "10" on question six, please let us know and give us the opportunity to grow and get better!
